SEO

Search Engine Optimisation is a marketing practice for gaining visibility for your website in search engine results. There are many aspects to SEO, from the words on your website to the way other sites link to you on the web. SEO is mainly a matter of making sure your site is structured in a way that search engines understand.

Social Media

Social media marketing is the process of gaining traffic to your website or awareness through social media channels, such as Facebook, Twitter etc. Social media marketing programs usually centre on efforts to create content that attracts attention and encourages readers to share it with their social circles.

Analytics

Analytics is data collection and management. Google Analytics provides an accurate view of the customer. You can find out who your target audience are, whats your most effective marketing channels and more. This tool will help you tailor a great marketing / business strategy.

PPC

Google AdWords is an advertising platform that offers a Pay-Per-Click service. These adverts are displayed on Google’s search results above all organic website listings. PPC is great for capturing the people that know what they are looking for, we just need to ensure your advert is there when they look for you over your competitors.

Email Service

Online email marketing that manages your subscribers, send’s emails, and track the results. Working with various platforms we can ensure your emails are branded, optimised for all devices and of a high quality considering the customer and your marketing strategy.
